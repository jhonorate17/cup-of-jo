Hello! My name is Joel Honorate — feel free to call me Jo.
I’m a junior at the University of North Carolina at Asheville, majoring in New Media with a minor in Computer Science.

My focus areas are 3D modeling, interactive media, and creative coding.

I’ve been in love with movies, video games, and the digital art world for as long as I can remember. That love began when I first sat down at a computer as a kid in Cuba.

After graduating high school, I initially attended UNC Charlotte as a business major. It felt like the expected and easy path at the time — so I followed it. Very quickly, I realized I was deeply uninterested and struggling academically.

I stepped away from school, worked a blue-collar job, and spent about a year thinking seriously about what I wanted from my life. Eventually, I chose to embrace the creative side of myself that I had kept tucked away — a side I was admittedly scared to bring into the light.

That decision led me to UNC Asheville, where I discovered a space that values creativity and experimentation!
